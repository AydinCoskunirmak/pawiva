# Pawiva – Privacy Policy

Last Updated: 01.05.2026

Pawiva ("the App", "we", "our", "us") values your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and protect your information when you use the App. By using Pawiva, you consent to the practices described in this Privacy Policy.

## 1. Information We Collect

We may collect the following types of information:

- **Activity Data:** Information you provide when recording pet-related activities (e.g., walking time, playtime, sleeping time).
- **Account Information:** If you create an account, we may collect your email address or other identifiers you provide.
- **Device Information:** Basic technical information such as device type, operating system, app version, and usage statistics.

Pawiva does not collect or process health-related data (e.g., vaccinations, medical treatments, or medical history).

## 2. How We Use Your Information

- To provide, operate, and improve the App.
- To generate analytics and insights about your pet activities.
- To allow you to share selected activity data on social media platforms (if you choose to do so).
- To communicate with you regarding updates, support, or important notices.

## 3. Sharing of Information

We do not sell or rent your personal data to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

- With service providers who support our operations (e.g., analytics or cloud hosting providers).
- If required by law or to protect our legal rights.
- With your consent, when you choose to share your activity data on third-party platforms (e.g., Instagram).

## 4. Data Storage & Security

- Your data is stored securely using industry-standard practices.
- While we take reasonable measures to protect your information, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.
- You are responsible for keeping your account credentials confidential.

## 5. Your Rights

Depending on your jurisdiction, you may have the right to:

- Access the personal data we hold about you.
- Request correction or deletion of your data.
- Withdraw your consent for data processing (where applicable).

To exercise these rights, please contact us at csknrmk.aydn@icloud.com

## 6. Children's Privacy

Pawiva is not directed at children under the age of 13 (or the age of digital consent in your country). We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

## 7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Changes will be posted within the App, and the updated policy will become effective upon posting.
